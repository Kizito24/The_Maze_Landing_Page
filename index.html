About The Maze

'The Maze' is a thrilling 3D maze game developed using raycasting technology. Dive into the heart-pounding gameplay, navigate intricate mazes, solve puzzles, and escape before time runs out. Every step could be your last!

Features

  • Classic raycasting visuals for a retro experience.
  • Intricate maze designs with increasing difficulty.
  • Challenging puzzles and hidden paths.
  • Multiple levels and game modes.
  • Intuitive controls for smooth gameplay.
